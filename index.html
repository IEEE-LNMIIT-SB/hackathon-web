Details

What is a Hackathon?

Hack@Plinth is a student competition to build the most exciting technology products within a timeframe of 24 hours. So think, design and develop, And don't forget to enjoy.



Who can attend?

All students are welcome! This includes university undergrads, graduate students, and high school students. We welcome complete beginners eager to write their first lines of code and experienced programmers alike!


How big are teams?

Teams may contain up to 5 people. If you don't have a team prior to the event, that's completely fine - we will be holding a team-matching session at the beginning of the hacking period. You can also compete alone, but that's a lot less fun!

What can my team build?

Whatever hardware, software, or technology-related projects you and your team can come up with! Projects will be judged based on creativity, technical difficulty, polish, and usefulness.




How much does it cost to attend?

Registration Cost for First member of team is Rs. 500, and for all other team members Rs. 200 per member. This includes food and drinks.



What should I bring?

All attendees are required to bring their valid student ID for admission (name must match the application). If you want to do a hardware hack, don't forget to bring the devices you plan on working with just in case we run out hardware to loan out.
